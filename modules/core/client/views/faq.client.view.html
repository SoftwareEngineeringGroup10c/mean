General

What is Agora?

Agora is a software platform that centralizes communication for organizations. With Agora, you no longer have to use multiple apps to communicate with your members. Announcements, Events, Polls, Attendance Tracking, Document Sharing, Member Directories - Agora has it all!
AgoraOrg.com

Who is Agora for?

Agora is for anyone involved with an organization! Fraternities, sororities, honors clubs, non-profits, sports teams, etc... Any type of organization can benefit using Agora.

What is Agora Fundraising?

Agora Fundraising is a web application that stems from Agora. It allows student organizations to connect with businesses in order to host upcoming fundraising events.

I forgot my password. What can I do?

To reset your password: First open Agora Fundraising and navigate to the login page by tapping on "Log in". Under "Restore your Password," enter your account username, and an email will be sent the email address associated with the account.

Student Organizations

Why would a student organization sign up for Agora Fundraising?

Student organizations would be able to connect with local businesses in order to host a fundraising night. Users would be able to view available events from various businesses, request an event, and cancel requests.

How can I sign up my student organization?

There are two ways to sign up your organization on Agora:
1. Click the "Sign Up" button at the top right hand side of this page.
2. Click the "Dashboard" button and you will be prompted to either log in or create an account.

Can other members join my account in Agora Fundraising?

No, there should only be one account associated with each student organization account. This is to avoid any confusion that could arise from multiple users. However, an user can transfer accounts to another email address by novering over your account picture on the upper right corner, navigating to "Settings", and inputting the new email address.

How can I request an event?

Once logged in to your student organization account, select the dashboard tab. Select "Request Event" to view a list of available fundraising days from local businesses. Hit the "Request Event" button to send that business a notification for that event. You may also cancel your request in this same lcoation.

Businesses

Why would a business sign up for Agora Fundraising?

Businesses would be able to connect with the University of Florida in order to host a fundraising night. Users would be able to view create new events, view requests for an event, accept a student organization for the event, and delete an event.

How can I create an event?

Once logged in to your business account, select the dashboard tab. Select "Create Event", fill out all the required information about the upcoming fundraising event, and select an image for the event banner. Select "Create Event" to save the information you just entered.

How can I change my profile picture?

Select your account on the upper right corner, and hit the "Settings" option on the dropdown list. Under "Edit Profile," there will be the option to change your profile picture. Hit "Select Image" and choose an picture from your comptuer. Hit "Upload" to finalize the profile picture.

How can I change my email?

Select your account on the upper right corner, and hit the "Settings" option on the dropdown list. Under "Edit Profile," there will be the option to change your email on the right side of the screen. Input the new email address that you want to change it to and select "Update Profile".
